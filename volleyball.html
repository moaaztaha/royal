Info:


Coaches

We have a number of national champions training as coaches, so that we can provide quality training for upcoming champions or in other words the kids/youth new to the volleyball world.Our coaches devote themselves to discover new talents and give them the required training to master the volleyball game.

Team

Our team is chosen every year to choose the best players to compete under the name of Royal Champions.Furthermore, each team member is payed a monthly salary as a way of motivation. Our team compete in both local and universal championships and have won various championships and thus, our team is known globally.

Sponsors

Our sponsors are well-known companies that provide us with high quality equipments to offer the best experience for Royal Champions players. Our sponsors include "mikasa" which is the company which provides the best volleyballs in the world.Being offered these high quality equipments gives players an image of how a real championship would look like.
